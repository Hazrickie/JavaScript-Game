How To Play




In this game, you need to defend your base from getting attacked by the robots.



There are 3 level:


The Laboratory - Easy

City - Medium

Hell - Hard




To place tower, first click on the tower option. There are three types of tower:


Turret - Normal tower, nothing special

Rocket - Dealt more damage than turret

Farm - Gives 20 gold every 20 seconds
